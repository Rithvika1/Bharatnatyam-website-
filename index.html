About Us

Rashmi is a dedicated Bharatanatyam performer and tutor with years of experience teaching students of all age groups. Her classes focus on rhythm, expression, technique, and the rich cultural storytelling that makes Bharatanatyam so unique.

Whether you’re just starting or looking to master advanced techniques, Rashmi’s structured yet nurturing style helps every student grow with confidence and grace.

Our Classes & Schedule

We offer structured programs with an emphasis on:

  • Adavus (basic steps)
  • Postures and Mudras (hand gestures)
  • Facial Expressions (Abhinaya)
  • Jathis and Korvais (rhythmic sequences)
  • Classical compositions and repertoire
  • Stage Performance Preparation
